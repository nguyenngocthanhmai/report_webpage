Group Processes

In the Project proposal, our result was not high because we suffered from many misunderstandings. Nonetheless, we are determined to try our best to enhance the score in this upcoming assignment. Yet we are worried because Our IT World project has a large scale and the challenges that first-year students face. There are many difficult requirements that we are not accustomed to doing, such as writing reports or creating a website. Fortunately, we worked together in the Project proposal, so everything runs straightforward in our initial discussion for this assignment via a face-to-face meeting at the university. We also consulted with each other during the project process through Ms. Teams and direct meetings at school once a week for the members to raise questions that they have while working on the project. Besides, on our Git Hub group repository, which we use to create a website showcase our expected product using HTML we had learned in tutorial class, our group worked collaboratively and harmonically.
