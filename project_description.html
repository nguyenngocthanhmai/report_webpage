Project Description

Overview

  • Topic

    • In the first assignment, our team had an idea about creating an AI-based autonomous wheelchair from natural materials. However, that topic was too advanced for freshmen like us to complete all requirements for producing a practical AI wheelchair. Therefore, in the Our IT World project, we decide to develop a website to showcase our expected product to potential investors and customers. Our team put necessary elements into our website, such as a Home page, a Our product page (displays the features and components used), a About us page (introduces our team and each members), and a Contact us page. We guarantee that while poking around the website, customers can view the provided detailed information, understand the vision of why our team carries out the AI-based autonomous wheelchair for disabled people, and they will consider buying our product.
    Besides, we will concentrate mostly on the must-have function which is Our Product section. In this section, customers will be able to see the overview of our wheelchair. The vehicle information has been primarily displayed like a portfolio, which contains explanation text lines for every feature including sensors, monitor, and headsets, in parallel with the illustration images. Hence, it allows clients to easily visualize the practical wheelchair after approaching our website. For the good outcome, we decide to use both HTML and CSS tool to complete all developing website requirements from writing codes, customizing, and executing the website.

  • Motivation

    • Our team began with an aim to create an IT product that would benefit the society. However, afterwards we shifted our focus to disabled people, as we were deeply moved by the story about their life. A large number of handicapped individuals in developing countries, such as Vietnam, are going through a lot of difficulty in travelling around due to the not fully developed road infrastructure. The notion of automating vehicle is one of the most popular IT trends in the world at the moment. A lot of ‘giant’ corporations, such as Tesla, Google or Intel are all competing in this fierce race. We believe our product could stand out from other similar systems.

  • Landscape

    • Virtually every business today has a website of their own to showcase their product because it is very important that the customers know what services they provide. However, in the field of transportation, some of the famous brands that we know are Vinfast and Toyota. While poking around these websites, they not only teach us how to use websites to implement organizational strategies, but they also orient us in the direction of methods to convert our website development beliefs into practice.


    * Being one of the most well-known car brands in Viet Nam, Toyota, of course, has a meticulous website with a variety of details, from product specifics and brand history to information on car services containing maintenance, preservation, and discount promotion.


    * Vinfast is well-known for being a rookie in the car industry. As a result, their website appears to be more innovative and up to date. In particular, the features of the website are dynamic with a variety of additional illustrations like gifs, VR, and AR integrated.

  • Plan and Progress

    • A. Plan

    We intend to create an electric AI-based wheelchair from natural material.
    We would go through 4 major steps to accomplish this goal:

    1.Use simulation to train AI how to travel on the road.


    The software that we would be using is VISTA 2.0. It is an open source driving simulator recently introduced to general public by MIT scientists, which provides training data and a testing environment for people interested in developing automated vehicles. This software is among the most sophisticated ones available because it provides hyper-realistic virtual world and can even simulates several sensors that are used in autonomous transportation.

    Figure 1 – VISTA 2.0 simulator
    Source: VISTA, 2022

    2.Create a connection link between the mind-reading device and AI.


    The mind-reading device we would be using is AlterEgo headset which is invented by scientists from MIT University. AI is embedded in a computer. AlterEgo is reported to be capable of interacting with AI-based appliances. Therefore, a connection between the mind-reading gadget and AI in the computer could be established. Any instructions made in the user’s mind could be transferred to AI to carry out.

    Figure 2 – AlterEgo headset
    Source: MIT Media Lab, 2019

    3.Build a physical wheelchair:


    This is a demonstration image of how the wheelchair should be like.


    Figure 3 - Example product image
    Source: Orange Badge


    - A computer is placed on a support stand behind the chair. The computer used is IP69K of Teguar Computers. We use this product because it is especially suitable for use in outdoor condition: waterproof, corrosion-resistant, antibacterial, and can expect a ten-year lifespan.


    Figure 4 - An IP69K waterproof computer
    Source: Teguar Computers

    - For the computer to see the surrounding: use cameras.
    * On the computer itself, there are two broad-view cameras, one at the front and the other at the back.
    * Around the wheelchair, we attach four medium-range cameras on the four sides of the chair.

    - However, a camera alone may make it hard for the computer to fully acknowledge the world, therefore we would use sensors to augment this understanding.
    * Lidar (light detection and ranging): monitor the vehicle’s surroundings.
    * Ultrasonic sensors: to measure the position of objects very close to the vehicle.

    - GPS would be installed in our computer, the map used is high-definition (HD) map.

    - Other physical components of our product are similar to those of a standard electric wheelchair, which has a knob on the armrest for vehicle control, except that the chassis of model is made from biocomposite. In order to produce this electric wheelchair, we would search for a manufacturer who is a specialist in this area.

    - We also install the shock absorber for the wheels. Whenever the wheels are rolling along the street, it cannot avoid vibrations. In some cases, users have no choice but to travel through bumpy roads if they are to reach their destination. A “shock absorber” would support them in minimizing shock and vibrations caused to the wheels during the journey. This product would help to maintain the accurate operation of electrical components (and therefore ensures the safety of users), which in the long run may also reduce maintenance costs and downtime.

  • Scope and Limits

    • Scope

    Our team understands thoroughly that an Electric AI-based autonomous wheelchair is not familiar to the majority of people. Clients will naturally feel hesitant whether to buy our product or not. Hence, we want to solve that problem by developing a website, which provides the sufficient necessary information to help user understand and familiarize themselves with our product.

      Our website has the similar interface of other fundamental websites including the home page, our product, about us, and contact section
    • Home page : This is an essential feature of our website because clients initially approach it. In there, we have some fundamental information, such as the logo, the slogan, the product name, and the navigation bar.
    • Our Product : This is the most crucial section on our website. In this section, there are details about our product “An electric wheelchair assisted by AI” containing sufficient technical parameters, images, and description.
    • About Us : This page will provide customers with website founders' information. In addition, we also add the inspiration behind our team’s name. When scrolling down, there are four boxes that display the details of each team member.
    • Contact Us : we create this section to help customers connect straight with us. We design some boxes where clients fill out their personal information from first and last names, email, and phone numbers to messages for my team. Some connect details are available on this page, we provide the address, email, telephone number, and location through Google Maps.


    Limits

    Being first-year students, we confront many problems during the website development process. Hence, our website would be limited in some features because those are out of our developing capabilities.
    * Contact section: although customers can feel free to fill out their personal information to our box, submission action is unsuccessful (the website is static, the information that they fill in are not saved to the server, therefore it will disappear after they reload the page).
    * We initially plan to develop an e-commerce website, which includes order and booking section for our products. However, these ideas are complex to us, so we do not carry out it yet. Therefore, we choose to showcase our ideal product as the style of a portfolio.
